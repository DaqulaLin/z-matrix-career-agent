About Qunshu Zhiyao
Qunshu Zhiyao is essentially the ultimate Executive Briefing curated for the C-suite of ancient empires. Compiled during the Tang Dynasty—China’s golden age of prosperity—it is a masterfully condensed anthology of leadership wisdom spanning over thousands of years.

Instead of making the busy Emperor read thousands of historical volumes, his top advisors distilled the absolute essentials on crisis management, talent acquisition, organizational alignment, and sustainable growth. In today’s corporate world, it serves as an unparalleled strategic blueprint for CEO/Executive roles on how to foster organizational synergy and lead with high emotional and ethical intelligence.
About Guoxue Zhiyao
Guoxue Zhiyao is a comprehensive intellectual roadmap, akin to the Western 'Great Books' movement. Compiled in the early 20th century to preserve the bedrock of Chinese civilization, it organizes the vast sea of Chinese classics into a structured framework: Philosophy, History, Statecraft, and Literature.

For a global professional, this book is a masterclass in systems thinking and cultural intelligence (CQ). It teaches you how the Chinese mind navigates long-term vision (History), ethical frameworks (Philosophy), and persuasion (Literature). It is the perfect tool for Chief Strategy Officers or HR Leaders who need to build a holistic mindset and manage complex, multi-dimensional ecosystems.
